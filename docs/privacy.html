Privacy Policy

It Counts For You is a simple counter app. This policy explains what data the app collects — which is none.

Data collection

It Counts For You does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. There are no user accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party tracking of any kind.

Where your data lives

All counters and app data you create stay on your device, saved locally. Nothing is sent to a server because the app doesn't have one.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be raised via the project's GitHub repository.
